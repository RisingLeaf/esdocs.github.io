Detailed Description

Class representing a projectile (a moving object which can hit ships or asteroids and can potentially be hit by anti-missile systems). A projectile may either move at a constant heading and velocity, or may accelerate or change course to track its target. Also, when they hit their target or reach the end of their lifetime, some projectiles split into "sub-munitions," new projectiles that may look different or travel in a new direction.
