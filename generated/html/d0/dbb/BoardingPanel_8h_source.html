33// This panel is displayed whenever your flagship boards another ship, to give
34// you a choice of what to plunder or whether to attempt to capture it. The
35// items you can plunder are shown in a list sorted by value per ton. Ship
36// capture is "turn-based" combat where each "turn" one or both ships lose crew.
