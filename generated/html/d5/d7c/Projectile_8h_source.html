35// Class representing a projectile (a moving object which can hit ships or
36// asteroids and can potentially be hit by anti-missile systems). A projectile
37// may either move at a constant heading and velocity, or may accelerate or
38// change course to track its target. Also, when they hit their target or reach
39// the end of their lifetime, some projectiles split into "sub-munitions," new
40// projectiles that may look different or travel in a new direction.
