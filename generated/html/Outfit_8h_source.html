36// Class representing an outfit that can be installed in a ship. A ship's
37// "attributes" are simply stored as a series of key-value pairs, and an outfit
38// can add to or subtract from any of those values. Weapons also have another
39// set of attributes unique to them, and outfits can also specify additional
40// information like the sprite to use in the outfitter panel for selling them,
41// or the sprite or sound to be used for an engine flare.
