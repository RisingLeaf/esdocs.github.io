40// A GameAction represents what happens when a Mission or Conversation reaches
41// a certain milestone. This can include when the Mission is offered, accepted,
42// declined, completed, or failed, or when a Conversation reaches an "action" node.
43// GameActions might include giving the player payment or a special item,
44// modifying condition flags, or queueing a GameEvent to occur. Any new mechanics
45// added to GameAction should be able to be safely executed while in a
46// Conversation.

48public:
49 GameAction() = default;
50 // Construct and Load() at the same time.
