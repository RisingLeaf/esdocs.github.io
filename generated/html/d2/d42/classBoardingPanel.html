Detailed Description

This panel is displayed whenever your flagship boards another ship, to give you a choice of what to plunder or whether to attempt to capture it. The items you can plunder are shown in a list sorted by value per ton. Ship capture is "turn-based" combat where each "turn" one or both ships lose crew.
