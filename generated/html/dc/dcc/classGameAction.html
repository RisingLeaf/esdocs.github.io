Detailed Description

A GameAction represents what happens when a Mission or Conversation reaches a certain milestone. This can include when the Mission is offered, accepted, declined, completed, or failed, or when a Conversation reaches an "action" node. GameActions might include giving the player payment or a special item, modifying condition flags, or queueing a GameEvent to occur. Any new mechanics added to GameAction should be able to be safely executed while in a Conversation.
